• Alu Sadheko

    1. Ingredients
      • Boiled potatoes
      • Finely chopped onion
      • Sliced green chilies
      • Tomato puree
      • Chopped coriander
      • Turmeric powder
      • Cumin powder
      • Chili powder
      • Fenugreek seeds
      • Lemon juice
      • Mustard oil
      • Salt to taste
    2. Recipe
      • Cut boiled potatoes into cubes.
      • Mix potato, onion, tomato puree, chilies, cumin, chili powder, salt, lemon juice, and coriander.
      • Heat mustard oil, add fenugreek seeds.
      • When browned, add turmeric and pour over potato mix.
      • Mix well and serve.
