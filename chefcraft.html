1. Yomari
    • Ingredients
      • Rice flour
      • Chaku (molasses)
      • Sesame seeds
      • Ghee
      • Coconut
    • Recipe
      • Heat chaku with sesame until liquid.
      • Make dough with rice flour and warm water.
      • Shape into a cone.
      • Fill chaku mixture.
      • Steam for 10 minutes.
      • Enjoy!
  1. Sel Roti
    • Ingredients
      • Soaked rice
      • Sugar
      • Cardamom (Elaichi)
      • Ghee/Butter
      • Oil
      • Warm water
      • Baking soda
      • Suji & refined wheat flour
    • Recipe
      • Drain and grind the rice.
      • Add ghee, cardamom, sugar and mix well.
      • Rest the batter for 15–20 minutes.
      • Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
      • Pour batter in a ring shape.
      • Fry until golden brown.
      • Remove and drain excess oil.
      • Your Sel Roti is ready!
  1. Alu Sadheko
    • Ingredients
      • Boiled potatoes
      • Finely chopped onion
      • Sliced green chilies
      • Tomato purée
      • Chopped coriander
      • Turmeric powder
      • Cumin powder
      • Chili powder
      • Fenugreek seeds
      • Lemon juice
      • Mustard oil
      • Salt to taste
    • Recipe
      • Cut boiled potatoes into cubes.
      • Mix potato, onion, tomato purée, chilies, cumin, chili powder, salt, lemon juice, and coriander.
      • Heat mustard oil, add fenugreek seeds.
      • When browned, add turmeric and pour over potato mix.
      • Mix well and serve.
  1. Nepali Style Achar
    • Ingredients
      • Radish
      • Carrot
      • Cucumber
      • Boiled potato
      • Sesame seeds
      • Mustard oil
      • Fenugreek seeds
      • Green chilies
      • Salt
      • Turmeric powder
      • Chili powder
      • Cumin powder
    • Recipe
      • Cut radish, carrot, and cucumber into strips.
      • Cut boiled potatoes into cubes.
      • Roast sesame seeds and grind them.
      • Mix all vegetables, sesame powder, spices, salt, and chilies.
      • Heat oil, add fenugreek until brown.
      • Add turmeric and pour over mixture.
      • Mix well and serve.
