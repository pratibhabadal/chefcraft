1. Yomari
    • Ingredients
    • Rice flour
    • Chaku (molasses)
    • Sesame seeds
    • Ghee
    • Coconut
  2. Recipe
  3. Heat chaku with sesame until liquid.
  4. Make dough with rice flour and warm water.
  5. Shape into a cone.
  6. Fill chaku mixture.
  7. Steam for 10 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
  1. Sel Roti
    • Ingredients
    • Soaked rice
    • Sugar
    • Cardamom (Elaichi)
    • Ghee/Butter
    • Oil
    • Warm water
    • Baking soda
    • Suji & refined wheat flour
  2. Recipe
  3. Drain and grind the rice.
  4. Add ghee, cardamom, sugar and mix well.
  5. Rest the batter for 15–20 minutes.
  6. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  7. Pour batter in a ring shape.
  8. Fry until golden brown.
  9. Remove and drain excess oil.
  10. Your Sel Roti is ready!

    • Boiled potatoes

    • Sliced green chilies
    • Tomato purée
    • Chopped coriander
    • Turmeric powder
    • Cumin powder
    • Chili powder
    • Fenugreek seeds
    • Lemon juice
    • Mustard oil
    • Salt to taste
  1. Recipe
  2. Cut boiled potatoes into cubes.
  3. Mix potato, onion, tomato purée, chilies, cumin, chili powder, salt, lemon juice, and coriander.

  6. Mix well and serve.

    Nepali Style Achar

    • Ingredients
    • Radish
    • Carrot
    • Cucumber
    • Boiled potato
    • Sesame seeds
    • Mustard oil
    • Fenugreek seeds
    • Green chilies
    • Salt
    • Turmeric powder
    • Chili powder
    • Cumin powder
  1. Recipe
  2. Cut radish, carrot, and cucumber into strips.
  3. Cut boiled potatoes into cubes.
  4. Roast sesame seeds and grind them.
  5. Mix all vegetables, sesame powder, spices, salt, and chilies.
  6. Heat oil, add fenugreek until brown.
  7. Add turmeric and pour over mixture.
  8. Mix well and serve.
  1. Mixed Chatamari
    • Ingredients
    • Rice Flour
    • Water
    • Salt
    • Onion
    • Tomato
    • Coriander
    • Green Chillies
    • Egg/Meat
  2. Recipe
  3. Make a smooth batter with rice flour, water, and salt.
  4. Heat a pan and pour the batter thinly.
  5. Add the toppings i.e onion,tomato,coriander,chillies,egg/meat.
  6. Dont flip and cook!
  7. Cook in medium to low heat by covering.
  8. Enjoy your food.
