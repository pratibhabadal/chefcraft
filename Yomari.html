• Yomari

    1. Ingredients
      • Rice flour
      • Chaku (molasses)
      • Sesame seeds
      • Ghee
      • Coconut
    2. Recipe
      • Heat chaku with sesame until liquid.
      • Make dough with rice flour and warm water.
      • Shape into a cone.
      • Fill chaku mixture.
      • Steam for 10 minutes.
      • Enjoy!
