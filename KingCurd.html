• King Curd(Juju Dhau)

    1. Ingredients
      • Full Fat Milk
      • Active Yogurt starter
      • Sweetner
      • Cardamom powder
    2. Recipe
      • Boil milk with sugar ,cardamom and reduce.
      • Let milk cool to lukewarm.
      • Whisk the starter with little warm milk ,then stir it gently into the main pot.
      • Pour into ceramic bowl or clay pot.Cover anad wrap in a thick cloth or blanket.
      • Keep in warm spot for 6 to 8 hours without moving it.
      • Refrigerate for 2 hour before serving to firm it up.
      • Enjoy your king curd.
