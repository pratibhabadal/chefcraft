• Syaphale

    1. Ingridients
      • All Purpose flour(Maida)
      • Water
      • Salt
      • Meat(chicken/Buff)
      • Finely Chopped Onion
      • Ginger Garlic Paste
      • Soy Sauce
      • Chopped Corriander
      • Oil
      • Turmeric Powder, Cumin Powder, Chilli Powder
    2. Recipe
      • Mix flour, salt, and water to make soft dough. Rest it for 20 mins.
      • Mix all fillings ingredients properly.
      • Divide dough into small balls and roll into small circles.
      • Put fillings in center,fold into half-moon shape and seal edges tightly.
      • Deep fry in medium heat until golden brown.
      • Serve hot with achar or sauce.
