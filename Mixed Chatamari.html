• Mixed Chatamari

    1. Ingredients
      • Rice Flour
      • Water
      • Salt
      • Onion
      • Tomato
      • Coriander
      • Green Chillies
      • Egg or Meat
    2. Recipe
      • Make a smooth batter with rice flour, water, and salt.
      • Heat a pan and pour the batter thinly.
      • Add the onion,tomato,coriander,chillies,meat.
      • Cook in medium to low heat by covering.
      • Enjoy your food.
