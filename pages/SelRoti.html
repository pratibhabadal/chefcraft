Sel Roti Recipe

Click the image to view the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying

Recipe

  1. Mix rice flour, sugar, milk and ghee into a smooth batter.
  2. Add cardamom powder.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan.
  4. Pour batter in a circular ring shape.
  5. Fry until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm.
