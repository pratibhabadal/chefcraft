Thukpa Recipe

Click the image to view the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Chicken or vegetables
  • Carrot
  • Cabbage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chicken or vegetable stock

Recipe

  1. Boil the noodles and keep aside.
  2. Cook onion, garlic and ginger.
  3. Add vegetables and chicken.
  4. Pour in the stock and boil.
  5. Add noodles and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.
