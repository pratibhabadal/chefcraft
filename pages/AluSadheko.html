Alu Sadheko Recipe

Click the image to view the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Boiled potatoes
  • Finely chopped onion
  • Sliced green chilies
  • Tomato puree
  • Chopped coriander
  • Turmeric powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Chili powder
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Lemon juice
  • Mustard oil
  • Salt to taste

Recipe

  1. Cut boiled potatoes into cubes.
  2. Mix potatoes with onion, tomato puree, chilies, cumin powder, chili powder, salt, lemon juice and coriander.
  3. Heat mustard oil.
  4. Add fenugreek seeds until brown.
  5. Add turmeric powder.
  6. Pour over the potato mixture.
  7. Mix well.
  8. Serve fresh.
