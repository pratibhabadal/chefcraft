Laphing Recipe

Click the image to view the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Mung bean flour
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Chili oil
  • Sesame seeds
  • Coriander

Recipe

  1. Mix flour with water.
  2. Cook until thick.
  3. Pour into a tray and cool.
  4. Cut into strips.
  5. Add garlic, soy sauce, vinegar and chili oil.
  6. Mix well and garnish with coriander.
