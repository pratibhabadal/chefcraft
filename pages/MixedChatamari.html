Mixed Chatamari Recipe

Click the image to view the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • Water
  • Egg
  • Minced chicken
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Coriander
  • Salt
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Prepare batter using rice flour and water.
  2. Spread batter on a hot pan.
  3. Add minced chicken, egg, onion and tomato.
  4. Cover and cook until done.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
