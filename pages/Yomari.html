Yomari Recipe

Click the image to view the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • Chaku (molasses)
  • Roasted sesame seeds
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Mix rice flour with warm water to make a soft dough.
  2. Prepare the filling by mixing chaku and sesame seeds.
  3. Shape the dough into a fish-like form.
  4. Fill with the chaku mixture.
  5. Seal the dough properly.
  6. Steam for 15–20 minutes.
