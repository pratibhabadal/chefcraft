Chicken Momo Recipe

Click the image to view the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Momo wrappers
  • Minced chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Coriander
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking oil

Recipe

  1. Mix chicken with onion, garlic, ginger, coriander, salt and pepper.
  2. Place filling inside each momo wrapper.
  3. Fold and seal the edges.
  4. Arrange momos in a steamer.
  5. Steam for 12–15 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with tomato achar.
