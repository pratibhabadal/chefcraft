Onion Pakoda Recipe

Click the image to view the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Gram flour (Besan)
  • Rice flour
  • Green chilies
  • Coriander
  • Turmeric powder
  • Chili powder
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Oil for frying

Recipe

  1. Slice onions thinly.
  2. Mix onions with gram flour, rice flour and spices.
  3. Add little water to make a thick mixture.
  4. Heat oil.
  5. Drop spoonfuls into hot oil.
  6. Fry until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with chutney.
