Kheer Recipe

Click the image to view the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 litre milk
  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Cashews
  • Almonds
  • Raisins

Recipe

  1. Boil the milk in a pan.
  2. Add washed rice and cook on low heat.
  3. Stir regularly until the rice becomes soft.
  4. Add sugar and cardamom powder.
  5. Add cashews, almonds and raisins.
  6. Cook for a few more minutes.
  7. Serve warm or chilled.
