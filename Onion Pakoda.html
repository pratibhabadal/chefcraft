• Onion Pakoda

    1. Ingredients
      • Onion
      • Potato
      • Cabbage
      • Corriander
      • Gram flour(Besan)
      • Ginger, Garlic, Green Chillies
      • Turmeric Powder
      • Cumin Seeds
      • Salt as per taste
      • Water
      • Oil
    2. Recipie
      • Slice onions thin and put in a bowl.
      • Add potato, cabbage if you want.
      • Add salt and mix well.Keep for 5 min(Onion and Cabbage release water).
      • Add Basen,Spiceis,Green chili,Ginger,Cumin seeds and Coriander.
      • Mix everything. Add very little if needed.
      • Heat oil in a pan.
      • Drop small portions into hot oil.
      • Fry on medium heat until golden and crispy.
      • Serve hot with ketchup or chutney.
